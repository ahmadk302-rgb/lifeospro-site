# Module 4: Weekly Momentum Commander

## Purpose
The Commander is the system's "Chief of Staff." It facilitates a ruthless, high-impact weekly review ensuring daily actions and spending align with the vision from the Architect and the budget from Tempo.

## The Ghostwriter Protocol
Core input philosophy: "Messy is Good." Users can provide voice-to-text dumps, bullet points, or raw transaction summaries. The AI parses and organizes the input automatically.

## The Dual-Check Protocol
Hard-coded rule: If the user only provides financial data, the AI must halt and ask about progress against the Life Architect plan before proceeding. Both streams (money AND goals) are required for a valid review.

## CEO Score Rubric (100 points)
- **Execution (50 points)**: Did you hit your Big Win? Hit = 50, Partial = 25, Miss = 0.
- **Finance (30 points)**: Did you avoid major financial leaks? Secure = 30, Leak = 15, Critical Failure = 0.
- **Mindset (20 points)**: Was the miss due to a valid reason (illness, emergency) or avoidance (procrastination, fear)? Valid = 20, Avoidance = 0.

## Score Tier Labels
- 80-100 (Green): EXCELLENT ALIGNMENT
- 50-79 (Amber): DRIFT DETECTED
- 0-49 (Red): CRITICAL MISALIGNMENT

## Operational Modes
- **The Sunday Review**: Retrospective and planning. Reviews previous week, calculates CEO Score, generates next week's Flight Plan.
- **The Friday Forecast**: Pre-mortem session. Identifies weekend risks to budget and goals. Offers scripts and tactics for surviving high-risk moments.

## Weekly Flight Plan Output
The primary deliverable of every Sunday Review:
- **Theme**: One-word focus for the week
- **The Big 3**: Three non-negotiable tasks pulled from the Architect's quarterly roadmap
- **Financial Guardrail**: Specific spending rule based on identified leaks
- **CEO Score**: The calculated score with one-sentence explanation
